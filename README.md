Shopgress
==============

Shopgress is an application for Android where users can create and manage their shopping lists.

Main features
------------------------------
*   Have all of your shopping lists in one place and easily update your progress
*   Look at visual statistics of how many items each list has and how many of them are resolved or unresolved
*   Archiving allows you to hide shopping lists that you don't want to see at the moment but plan on using them later
*   Invite friends or family members to a shopping list by searching their username

Light / Dark Mode
------------------------------
*   You can switch between light and dark mode in the top panel
*   Switching to dark mode is recommended when using the app in the evening or at night

Languages
------------------------------
*   Curretly supported languages are English and Czech
*   Just like mode, the current language can be changed in the top panel
